ABOUT US

At Patches Pub & Grill, we are all about creating an unforgettable experience for our customers. From our award-winning burgers to our lively atmosphere, we strive to make every visit special. Proudly voted Best Sports Bar, Best Wings, and Best Burgers from 2016 to 2023 by PC Living Magazine, we continue to set the standard for quality and service.

Whether you’re here to enjoy live local music, our award-winning food, or just want to catch a game with friends, Patches Pub & Grill offers the perfect place to relax, unwind, and indulge in great food. Our menu boasts signature dishes like juicy burgers with sides including french fries, sweet potato fries, mac & cheese, and more!

Join us and discover why we've been voted Best of Bay from 2016 through 2023. Our friendly staff, delicious meals, and lively atmosphere will keep you coming back for more.
